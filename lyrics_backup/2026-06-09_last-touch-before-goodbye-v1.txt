[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The porch light fades into the dark
I feel the beating of your heart
Just let the shadows pull us close
This is the moment I want most

[Verse 1]
We left the crowded room behind
A hidden space for us to find
Your fingers trace across my skin
[Whispered] (And let the quiet pull us in)

[Pre-Chorus]
The air is thick with sweet desire
We move a little near the fire
You lean your head against my chest
And let the silence do the rest

[Chorus]
I want to hold you through the night
Until the morning brings the light
There is a secret in your sigh
Just one last touch before goodbye

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, just a little closer now
[Echo] (Let me show you, let me show you how)

[Verse 2]
I hear the rhythm of your breath
And nothing else is left to guess
We trace the shadows on the floor
And leave our worries at the door

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The temperature begins to rise
I see the question in your eyes
We take a step into the haze
And we get lost inside this maze

[Chorus]
I want to hold you through the night
Until the morning brings the light
There is a secret in your sigh
Just one last touch before goodbye

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, just a little closer now
[Echo] (Let me show you, let me show you how)

[Bridge]
Just one last touch before goodbye
We let the world outside just die
The morning sun is out of sight
We have the cover of the night
I'll be the only one you need
Just leave it all up to Saeed

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (Look at the fire fading low)
[Spoken] (We still have so far left to go)

[Final Chorus]
I want to hold you through the night
Until the morning brings the light
There is a secret in your sigh
Just one last touch before goodbye
